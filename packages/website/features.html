Features

Assessment & Analysis Tools

Structured assessments and vulnerability analysis for vendor risk evaluation.

Supply Chain Risk Assessment

NIST SP 800-161 aligned assessments with 24 questions across 6 domains. Real-time scoring and recommendations.

SBOM Analysis

Real-time vulnerability analysis via OSV Database. Supports CycloneDX and SPDX formats. Component-level risk assessment with CVE mapping.

Vendor Risk Calculator

Evaluates 5 weighted factors to calculate real-time risk scores (0-100) with automatic classification and recommendations.

NIST Checklist Tool

10 NIST C-SCRM controls across 5 categories. Interactive checklist with progress tracking and exportable reports.

Vendor Management & Organization

Centralized vendor inventory and evidence collection.

Vendor Risk Management

Centralized vendor inventory with risk profiles and automated classification. Track compliance status and link to assessments.

Evidence Vault

Centralized document management with version control and audit trails. Link evidence to assessment questions and compliance controls.

Visualization & Scoring

Risk visualization and automated compliance scoring.

Vendor Risk Radar

Interactive radar charts for privacy-focused risk visualization. Maps to GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA with category-specific templates.

NIST Compliance Scoring

Automated compliance scoring across 5 categories with weighted percentages. Generates actionable recommendations based on compliance levels.

Workflow & Integration

Vendor collaboration tools and audit reporting.

Vendor Portal (VendorTal)

Self-service portal for vendor assessments. Vendors complete questionnaires independently; responses sync to your dashboard.

Reporting & Export

Audit reports for NIST SP 800-161, CMMC 2.0, and other frameworks. Export PDF and JSON formats with summaries, gap analysis, recommendations, and evidence inventories.
