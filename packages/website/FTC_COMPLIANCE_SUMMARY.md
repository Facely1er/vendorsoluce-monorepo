## üîç Key FTC Principles

1. **Truthfulness** - All claims must be honest
2. **Substantiation** - Must have evidence before publishing
3. **Typical vs. Exceptional** - Must distinguish between typical and best-case
4. **Clear Disclosures** - Disclaimers must be prominent
5. **No Overbroad Claims** - Cannot imply all customers get same results
