## Platform Overview

**VendorSoluce** is a NIST SP 800-161 compliant Supply Chain Risk Management platform offering:
- Real-time SBOM vulnerability analysis (OSV Database integration)
- Supply Chain Risk Assessments (24 NIST-aligned questions)
- Vendor Risk Management Dashboard
- Compliance Templates & Resources

**Premium Add-On (VendorTal):**
- Advanced Vendor Security Assessment Distribution System
- CMMC and NIST Privacy Framework assessments
- Vendor portal for assessment completion
- Automated compliance scoring and analytics

**Current Pricing:** $49 (Starter) → $149 (Professional) → $449 (Enterprise) → Custom (Federal)

## Pricing Optimization Recommendations

### Current Issues:
1. ❌ Large gap between tiers ($49 → $149 → $449)
2. ❌ No usage-based components
3. ❌ Vendor limits are restrictive (10 → 50 → Unlimited)
4. ❌ No annual discount incentive

### Recommended Changes:

#### New Pricing Structure:
```
Starter: $49/month or $490/year (save $98)
- 10 vendors
- 5 SBOM scans/month
- 1 user
- Email support
- $10 per additional vendor/month
- $5 per additional SBOM scan

Professional: $129/month or $1,290/year (save $258)
- 50 vendors
- 50 SBOM scans/month
- 5 users
- Priority support
- API access (10K calls/month)
- $5 per additional vendor/month
- $3 per additional SBOM scan
- $20 per additional user/month

Enterprise: $399/month or $3,990/year (save $798)
- Unlimited vendors
- Unlimited SBOM scans
- Unlimited users
- Dedicated support
- Custom integrations
- SSO/SAML
- SLA guarantee
- Volume discounts available

Federal: Custom Pricing
- All Enterprise features
- FedRAMP authorized
- GovCloud deployment
- CAC/PIV authentication
- Enhanced security controls
```

**Expected Impact:**
- Increase conversions by 25% (lower entry barrier)
- Increase ARPU by 30% (usage-based components)
- Increase annual prepay by 50% (clear savings)
