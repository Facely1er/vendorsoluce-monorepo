\## SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)\
\
\*\*Effective Date:\*\* \[[TIME OF PURCHASE]{.mark}\] \
\*\*Last Updated:\*\* October 31, 2025\
\
This Service Level Agreement ("SLA") defines the service level
commitments ERMITS LLC ("ERMITS") provides to customers using paid
subscription tiers. This SLA supplements the Master Terms of Service.\
\
\### 1.1 Scope and Applicability\
\
\*\*1.1.1 Covered Services\*\*\
\
This SLA applies to:\
\
- \*\*Professional Tier:\*\* All ERMITS products with paid Professional
subscriptions\
- \*\*Enterprise Tier:\*\* All ERMITS products with Enterprise
subscriptions\
- \*\*Federal/Government Tier:\*\* Custom government contracts (subject
to negotiated terms)\
\
\*\*1.1.2 Excluded Services\*\*\
\
This SLA does NOT apply to:\
\
- \*\*Free Tiers:\*\* Freemium accounts and free tools\
- \*\*Free Trials:\*\* Trial accounts during trial period\
- \*\*Beta Products:\*\* Products explicitly marked as Beta, Preview, or
Early Access\
- \*\*Discontinued Products:\*\* Products in sunset or deprecation
phase\

\### 1.2 Service Availability Commitment\
\
\*\*1.2.1 Uptime Guarantee\*\*\
\
ERMITS commits to the following uptime levels:\
\
\|Subscription Tier     \|Monthly Uptime Commitment \| Maximum Downtime
per Month \|\
\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|\
\|\*\*Professional\*\*      \|99.9%                    \|\~43 minutes

           \|\
\|\*\*Federal/Government\*\*\|99.95% (negotiable)      \|\~22 minutes   
           \|\
\
\*\*1.2.2 Uptime Calculation\*\*\
\
\*\*Uptime Percentage Formula:\*\*\
\
\`\`\`\
Uptime % = (Total Minutes in Month - Downtime Minutes) / Total Minutes
in Month × 100\
\`\`\`\
\
\*\*Example Calculation:\*\*\
\
- Month: January (44,640 minutes)\
- Downtime: 30 minutes\
- Uptime: (44,640 - 30) / 44,640 × 100 = 99.93%\
\
\*\*Measurement Period:\*\*\
\
- Calculated monthly (calendar month)\
- Measured in UTC timezone\
- Calculated per-product basis\
- Monitoring performed every 60 seconds\
\
\*\*1.2.3 Excluded Downtime\*\*\
\
The following are excluded from downtime calculations:\
\
\*\*Scheduled Maintenance:\*\*\
\
- Planned maintenance announced 48+ hours in advance\
- Scheduled during off-peak hours (typically 2-6 AM UTC)\
- Maximum 4 hours per month\
- Emergency security maintenance (with best-effort advance notice)\
\
\*\*Force Majeure Events:\*\*\
\
- Natural disasters, pandemics, acts of God\
- War, terrorism, civil unrest\
- Government actions or regulations\
- Internet backbone failures\
- Power grid failures affecting data centers\
\
\*\*Customer-Caused Issues:\*\*\
\
- User error or misconfiguration\
- Customer's network or internet connectivity issues\
- Customer's hardware or software failures\
- Unauthorized access to customer accounts\
- Exceeding allocated resource quotas\
\
\*\*Third-Party Service Failures:\*\*\
\
- Supabase infrastructure outages (beyond ERMITS control)\
- Stripe payment processing issues\
- DNS provider failures\
- CDN provider issues (Vercel)\
- OAuth provider outages (Google, Microsoft, GitHub)\
\
\*\*Beta Product Issues:\*\*\
\
- Any downtime related to Beta/Preview features\
- Testing or experimental functionality\
\

\### 1.5 SLA Credits and Remedies\
\
\*\*1.5.1 Service Credit Eligibility\*\*\
\
You are eligible for service credits if:\
\
- You have an active paid subscription (Professional, Enterprise, or
Federal tier)\
- Monthly uptime falls below committed percentage\
- You submit a claim within 30 days of the incident\
- Your account is in good standing (no payment issues)\
\
\*\*1.5.2 Service Credit Calculation\*\*\
\
\|Monthly Uptime Achieved\|Service Credit (% of Monthly Fee)\|\
\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|\
\|\< 99.9% but ≥ 99.0%    \|10%                              \|\
\|\< 99.0% but ≥ 98.0%    \|25%                              \|\
\|\< 98.0% but ≥ 95.0%    \|50%                              \|\
\|\< 95.0%                \|100%                             \|\
\
\*\*Example:\*\*\
\
- Monthly subscription: \$149\
- Uptime achieved: 98.5%\
- Service credit: \$149 × 25% = \$37.25\
\
\*\*1.5.3 Service Credit Limitations\*\*\
\
\*\*Maximum Credits:\*\*\
\
- Service credits cannot exceed 100% of monthly subscription fee\
- Credits calculated per product, not across entire account\
- Credits apply only to affected product/service\
\
\*\*Credit Application:\*\*\
\
- Credits applied to next month's invoice\
- Credits do not extend subscription term\
- Credits are not redeemable for cash\
- Credits expire if not used within 12 months\
\
\*\*1.5.4 Claiming Service Credits\*\*\
\
\*\*Claim Process:\*\*\
\
1. Submit claim via email: <contact@ermits.com>\
1. Subject: "SLA Credit Claim - \[Product Name\] - \[Month/Year\]"\
1. Include:\
- Account email and organization name\
- Product/service affected\
- Dates and times of downtime experienced\
- Description of impact\
- Supporting evidence (screenshots, logs if available)\
\
\*\*Processing:\*\*\
\
- Claims reviewed within 10 business days\
- Approval/denial notification via email\
- Credits applied to next invoice if approved\
- Disputes escalated to management review\
